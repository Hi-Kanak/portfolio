Alva

Alva, (A voice operated or voice activated assistant configurator) is an emerging technology that enhances the efficiency and user experience in various industrial and manufacturing settings.

This technology allows users to configure and control the voice activated machines and tools with their spoken commands and instruction, that makes easier to operate equipment and perform tasks.
