UX strategy

Design study

User Experience is one of the most important aspect of an application. However, inappropriate interfaces can easily cause users to leave. This application was previously based on a mental model of a developer, instead of a user which was the root case of different issues user was facing while navigating through the application.

  • The current user interface leaves users confused and bored in no time.
  • Its hard for user to understand the joruney at one go.
  • App lacks goal-oriented experiences causing cluelessness for users.
  • Task mechanism is not present in current application design
  • User Management System is not introduced with current design causing security risk and lack of usability.

Business goals and objective

To create experiences leveraging goal direction design method based on user motivation and objectives.

  • Enhance the ease of use by simplifying the application.
  • Group users and define their roles and activities.
  • Introduce the guided approach to help a user walk through a sequence of tasks on the way toward achieving that goal.
  • Define the notification system keeping all users updated and informed.

Course of Actions

I have followed the 4D process to create the seamless experiences for the user. The process is planned on four different phases.I and my team started extensive research of the business as well as users and target audience, understand their problems, pain points and closely observe the environment of workplace.

Stakeholder analysis

Discussions were held with stakeholders to get the glance of the business, tools, role of the tool to drive business,what are the important area to be addressed properly, what kind of changes they are envisioning.

Later, I talked to the domain experts to understand the problem space better. For instance, if I design the interface used by business professionals, I need to know the important steps of the process, the context, under what circumstances is it going to be used and so on.

Questionnaire

  • What will be different one year after this project is completed?
  • In what ways do you see the project helping you achieve your mission?
  • Which of these problems is the most important to solve?
  • What happens if this project is not done?
  • What tangible changes do you expect to see at the completion of this project?
  • How will you know if this project has been successfully completed?
  • What are the major risks to the project’s success?
  • What are the major obstacles or impediments to the success of this project?

Building persona

User Research was crucial to this project and one of the first things we did was to draft personas in collaboration with the client. We identified various users, their needs, motivations, and behaviours, and carefully studied theirexisting processes through telephonic and Skype interviews.

Using the results of the survey, we created three personas that embody the traits of the target audience.

Who are they ?

CTO, CFO, Account Manager, Product Owner, and others
Age:35-60 Years
Gender: Male and female
Family: Plural
Education: Degree, PG

Main Goals / What do they want ?

To learn new tools and technology for to serve better and improve work efficiency.
Make the decision better based on data available.


Pain-points / Frustration ?

Needs to take and make decision quicklybecause time is very important factor.
It takes much time to complete small task,there is not task mechanism approach.
Lacking Good quality of data
