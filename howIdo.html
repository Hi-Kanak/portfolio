How I do


Design is the science of the art. It is no longer only practice with the artistic way but there is also existence of scientific aspect.

When it comes to the business it solves the problems by enabling people, their lifestyles, emotions, habits and technology through the experience and experiments. I have evolved myself as scientific ux artist, focused in process-oriented approach. I truly believe that result is the byproduct of the process. Process is equally important as the result is.

Abstract to Concrete

As begin with design process I follow the mental framework called “The Golden Circle” which inspire the change by rearranging, how you approach the problem. The people start the design process by “what”, means the identifying things designing for. This method sets the limitation and reduce the flexibility towards innovation.

I start my work with identifying unknows which leads towards “why”

Methods and Procedure

Experience design lacks a one-size-fits-all benchmarking process, as its efficacy is contingent upon variables such as the nature of the business, geographical considerations, user demographics, and evolving technologies. The dynamic landscape of user experience undergoes substantial transformations propelled by the swift progress of technology. Over time, the shifting sands of human lifestyles contribute significantly to alterations in usability and learnability. This intricate interplay of factors underscores the need for a flexible and adaptive approach to experience design, one that embraces the dynamic nature of our evolving world.

I wholeheartedly believe that a well-crafted user experience serves as a catalyst in streamlining and simplifying the overall usability of a product or service.
