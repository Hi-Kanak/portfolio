Hygiea health


Hygiea health is an innovative and user-friendly mobile application that seamlessly combines comprehensive healthcare information with advanced medical insurance management.

With a user-centric design, Hygiea health aims to simplify the complex landscape of healthcare and insurance, making it easier for individuals and families to access quality care and manage their insurance needs effectively.

Personal Health and Insurance Profile

Create a detailed profile that includes your medical history, current health conditions, and insurance coverage information. This consolidated profile forms the basis for personalized healthcare recommendations and insurance solutions.

Appointment Scheduling

Book appointments with doctors, specialists, and healthcare facilities directly through the app. Receive appointment reminders and notifications to ensure you never miss an important medical visit.

Emergency Assistance and Locator

Find nearby hospitals, urgent care centres, and pharmacies using the app's integrated map feature. In case of emergencies, have quick access to important medical and insurance information, even from a locked device.

Medication Management

Set medication reminders, track dosages, and receive alerts for upcoming refills. Avoid missing doses and stay on top of your medication regimen effortlessly.
