Artful abode


A mobile app is designed to assist users in visualizing, customizing, and designing various types of doors for homes. This app allows users to explore different styles, materials, colors, and configurations of doors, helping them make informed decisions before purchasing or installing a door.

This app aims to provide a user-friendly and interactive platform for individuals, interior designers, architects, and contractors to experiment with door designs and make well-informed choices that align with their preferences and design goals.

Virtual door customization

You can choose from a range of door styles, such as traditional, modern, rustic, and more. You can then personalize aspects like door material, color, finish, panel design, and hardware like handles and locks.

Real-time visualization

You can visualize how the new designed door looks in their actual space. Device's camera are featured to superimpose the designed door onto the real environment, helping to gauge how it fits with the existing decor.

Model, measurement and sizing

The app include model selection, standard and customized sizing and measurement feature to help you accurately determine the dimensions of their door space, ensuring that the chosen door fits perfectly.

Integration with dealers

Configured design can be shared with local door dealers or direct manufacturers to directly purchase the designed door or receive a quote for the custom door.
