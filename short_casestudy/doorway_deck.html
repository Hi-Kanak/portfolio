Doorway deck


B2B Platform manages pricing, stocks, returns and other exceptions of various applications and is used by agencies, dealers and end users directly. User can upload configuration data, frame business rules and setup pricing.This tool is essential in driving the business seamlessly.

The challenges

The fundamental challenge for the company is the number of product returns or exchanges due to customer dissatisfaction and incorrect orders which resulted in increased costs, reduced customer satisfaction, and decreased profitability. They are expexting the solution which improves the overall experience, increase in orders and reduction in returns/exchanges.

The solutions

As a part of its tailored design approach, I investigated the case by discussion with stakeholders and conducting interviews at warehouse.I identified that inconsistent communication, old technology and complex customization process are the main hurdle.

After rigorous brainstorming came up with a solution, easy-to-use door configurator, where users can configure the doors by selecting the type of material, color and handles with an ease, along with it can virtually see how they door looks in real location. The users felt in love with this feature and were able to design and place order in very less time with minimal learning curve. This helped customers feel more confident in their purchasing decisions and reduce the likelihood of returns or exchanges due to confusion or misunderstandings.

The new design implementation has a profound and positive impact. Users are more likely to spend more time on the platform, explore different features, and interact with your content. Clear navigation, compelling calls-to-action, and a seamless user journey are helpful to accomplish the exact goal.
