Alva


The first universal, voice-enabled assistant technology specifically designed for manufacturing work. This revolutionary technology enables operators to interact with machine using simple voice commands and other methods of communication.

Significant barriers

Major business challenge for client is about learnability for users. Workflow is very complex. Itâ€™s difficult for user to understand and remember the entire process. It takes more time and effort to complete a task. This is impacting the overall productivity of the user. The legacy application is designed based on Windows 2000 pattern, this is one of the drawbacks working with the emerging technologies.

User centricity, simplicity and intuitiveness is missing in existing design. Application looks old-fashioned and not in line with modern design trends, making it less appealing to users.This is nothelping in marketing, Customer Acquisition and retention.

Unlocking the puzzle

Premilitary investigations like stakeholder discussion, business understanding, pain points analysis, are the initial actions of the ux strategy. Deep user analysis with various approach like user Interview, observation, contextual enquiry was the next step of the tailored process. Brainstorming session started after application review. I understood in and out, started to find out the possibility and feasibility of the modification. Also did some shot of Impact analysis of enhancement. Tasked based mechanism with progression introduced in the workflow and user journey also shorten here.

New idea facilitates user to create task and complete it through step-by-step navigation to accomplish the goal. Status notifies users, how much portion of task is completed and how much is pending. The new design empathizes the application and amplify the and look and feel.

As a result, the costs of support, promotion and learning has reduced, Productivity of the users has raised. Sales boosted and conversion rate also surged.
